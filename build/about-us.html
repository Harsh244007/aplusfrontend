Discover Our Story

A–Plus is one of India’s leading manufacturer of sound reinforcement products that deliver the most faithful and natural reproduction of sound in a variety of applications.
Established in 2002, A–Plus quickly established itself as a trusted brand in the sound reinforcement industry by developing products through continuous research & development that perform with absolute reliability even in the most extreme operating & climatic conditions.

Sonic Mastery: Our Unparalleled Specialty

The diverse portfolio of products comprises of Loud Speakers, Loud Speakers Systems, Amplifiers, Microphones, Mixers, etc.
Apart from offering a wide range of acoustic products, we also provide customized solutions to our clients through our team of qualified personnel so that they can enjoy the most remarkable sound just as they want.

Our Winning Formula

It is our mission to look at our business through their eyes, hence making us the market leader.
